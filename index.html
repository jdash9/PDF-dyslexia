How it works: The text is read from the PDF and re-set as clean, readable body text; the size differences between headings and body text are preserved. Columns, images and exact layout are not carried over. Works only with PDFs that have a real text layer — scanned ones would need OCR. Everything runs locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded.
